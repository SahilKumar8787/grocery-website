Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about Fresh Mart.

  • How do I place an order?
    Browse products, add to cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill your details and confirm payment.
  • What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept UPI, Google Pay, Visa, Mastercard, and Cash on Delivery.
  • How long does delivery take?
    Delivery usually takes 1-2 days within city limits. You’ll get updates via SMS.
  • Can I return or exchange products?
    Yes, you can return/exchange within 24 hours of delivery if the product is damaged or not fresh.
  • How do I contact support?
    Call us at +91 98765 43210 or email info@freshmart.com. We’re happy to help!
